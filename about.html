About

Amnesty International is a human rights organization that works to raise awareness and provide aid in order to put an end to issues from all over the world. Its main way of doing so is by raising awareness through a series of public service announcements.




About Us!

For this presentation, we will be interpreting different public service announcements put out by a single foundation (Amnesty International). We are analyzing them in this way in order to identify which platforms are most effective. We are presenting as “Ad Busters,” a group focused on proving how Amnesty International uses different media platforms to persuade viewers to help or join the cause. We used a website for our presentation to stay true to the premise and so that the information could be spread.
